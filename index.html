World Fair 64

A Text-Based Game of the 1964-1965 World's Fair

Type 'left', 'right', 'yes', and 'no' to navigate the avatar through the fairgrounds. There are three ways the game ends, when the avatar: runs out of money, runs out of time, or reaches the Lake Amusement Area or Transportation Area. The game starts once the player clicks 'Run'. Enjoy!
